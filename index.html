Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and products

What services does AuraLogic offer? +

AuraLogic offers comprehensive IT and AI solutions including Mobile App Development, AI-Based Solutions, Business Software Solutions, Complete IT Services, Systems Integration, and Web Development services. We specialize in creating custom solutions tailored to your business needs.

How can I join the early access program for your products? +

You can join our early access program by filling out the form in the "Join Our Early Access Program" section above. Simply provide your name, email, and select which product(s) you're interested in (TripMate, BuddyUp, or Dressera AI). We'll notify you when beta testing begins!

What technologies does AuraLogic use? +

We master a wide range of technologies including TensorFlow and PyTorch for AI/ML, React Native and Flutter for mobile development, React and Node.js for web development, and AWS and Azure for cloud infrastructure. Our tech stack is constantly evolving to include the latest innovations.

How much do your services cost? +

Our pricing varies based on project scope, complexity, and specific requirements. We offer custom solutions tailored to each client's needs and budget. To get an accurate quote, we recommend scheduling a free consultation where we can discuss your project in detail.

When will TripMate, BuddyUp, and Dressera AI be available? +

All three products are currently in development with a target launch in 2025. TripMate, BuddyUp, and Dressera AI will be available on iOS and Android app stores. Join our early access program to be among the first to try them!

Does AuraLogic offer support after project completion? +

Yes! We offer comprehensive post-launch support including maintenance, updates, troubleshooting, and 24/7 technical assistance. We believe in building long-term partnerships with our clients and ensuring their systems continue to perform optimally.

What industries do you work with? +

We work with businesses across various industries including technology, travel, healthcare, finance, retail, manufacturing, and more. Our solutions are designed to be adaptable and can be customized to meet the specific needs of any industry.

How long does a typical project take? +

Project timelines vary significantly based on scope and complexity. A simple mobile app might take 2-3 months, while comprehensive enterprise solutions can take 6-12 months or longer. We provide detailed project timelines during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the development process.
